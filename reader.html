a data visualization experiment
Upload a .txt or .html file. If you are using a .doc or .docx file, please use Microsoft Word's "Save as" and save your document as a web page. If your document is not displaying correctly, use the advanced options under "Save as" to ensure that your encoding is UTF-8.

Now partition your document. Click at any point in the text and hit return twice to create a partition. If you do not wish to create any more partitions, click Continue.
Now create sections in your partitions. Click at any point in the text and hit return twice to create a section. If you do not wish to create any more sections, click Continue.

Input your categories and choose a corresponding color for each category. Select a piece of text and click 1, 2, or 3 to highlight. To delete a category and its color, click on the circle next to the color. To delete a particular highlight, click on that highlight.
